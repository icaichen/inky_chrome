Privacy Policy – E-Ink Paper Mode (Chrome Extension)

Last updated:

Overview

E-Ink Paper Mode transforms webpages into a paper-like reading experience (Focus Mode / Kindle Mode), with optional color controls and a Safari-style Reader view. The extension runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.
  • No browsing history, keystrokes, or page contents are collected or transmitted.
  • No analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party scripts are used.

What We Store Locally

The extension uses chrome.storage to save your preferences locally on your device:

  • Current mode (Focus / Kindle / none)
  • Selected color option (Full / Soft / B&W)
  • Reader mode toggle

These settings never leave your device.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab – Apply or remove the reading/filter effects on the tab you are viewing.
  • storage – Persist your mode and color preferences across tabs/sessions.
  • scripting – Inject/remove the styles and overlays that create the paper effect.
  • webNavigation – Re-apply your chosen mode on SPA navigations and new page loads for consistency.
  • Host permissions (<all_urls>) – The extension must be able to adjust any site the user visits, to fulfill its single purpose.

Remote Code

The extension does not execute remote code. All scripts and assets are packaged inside the extension.

Children’s Privacy

The extension is a utility tool and does not target children or collect any personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or compliance updates. We will update the “Last updated” date above.
